Bridgerton

Overview Plot Cast Creative Team Characters Reviews

Overview

The eight close-knit siblings of the Bridgerton family look for love and happiness in London high society.

Inspired by Julia Quinn's bestselling novels.

Plot

Bridgerton follows Daphne Bridgerton (Phoebe Dynevor), the eldest daughter of the powerful Bridgerton family as she makes her debut onto Regency London's competitive marriage market.

Hoping to follow in her parent's footsteps and find a match sparked by true love, Daphne's prospects initially seem to be unrivaled.

Characters

  • Daphne Bridgerton: The eldest daughter of the Bridgerton family and the protagonist of the series. She is portrayed as intelligent, kind-hearted, and determined to find love in a society that values status and reputation above all else.
  • Anthony Bridgerton: Daphne's protective older brother and head of the Bridgerton family following their father's death. He is portrayed as a responsible but somewhat overbearing figure, especially when it comes to his sisters' romantic lives.
  • Simon Basset, Duke of Hastings: A close friend of Daphne's older brother, Simon is a brooding and enigmatic figure who is determined to remain a bachelor despite pressure from society to marry and produce an heir.
  • Lady Whistledown: A mysterious figure who anonymously publishes scandalous gossip about London's high society in a regular newsletter. Her identity is a closely guarded secret, and her writings have a significant impact on the lives of the characters.
  • Queen Charlotte: The queen consort of King George III and a prominent figure in London society. She takes an interest in the romantic lives of the characters and plays a role in the events of the series.
