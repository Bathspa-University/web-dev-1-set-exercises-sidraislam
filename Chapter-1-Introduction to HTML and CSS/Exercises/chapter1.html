Chicken Biryani

Biryani is a popular rice dish in south Asia. It is made with meat,rice and spices.

Ingredients Required

  • 1kg Chicken
  • Basmati Rice
  • 2 tsp ginger paste
  • 2 tsp garlic paste
  • yougurt 1 cup(200g)
  • Onion 3-4 medium
  • Tomato 2-3 medium
  • Green Chillies 4-5 medium
  • Cooking oil 1 cup(175ml)
  • Fresh corriander(for garnish)
  • Yellow food color 1 pinch
  • Biryani mix 1 packet (60g)

Cooking Method

  1. Fry onions in hot oil until brown,add Chickenc,ginger and garlic paste. stir fry for 5 mis. Add yougurt,biryani mix.Stir fry on medium heat.
  2. Add 1 cup of water. Cover and cook on low heat until the meat is done
  3. Add tomatoes and green Chillies. stir fry on high heat until the oil seperates.
  4. In a seperate pot, add rice add rice in12-13 cups of boiling water,stir in 3 tablespoons of salt boil rice until 3/4 cooked.
  5. Spread half rice in another pot and pour meat curry over it.Top with remaining rice.Pour yellow food color. Cover and simmer on low heat until the rice is tender.
  6. Mix before serving.Serve hot with salad and raita.
